# signal-progress

<LiveExample tag="signal-progress" />

Linear, ring, or spinner progress indicator.

## Props

- `value?: number` (default `0`)
- `max?: number` (default `100`)
- `indeterminate?: boolean`
- `type?: "bar" | "ring" | "spinner"` (default `"bar"`)
- `label?: string`
- `showValue?: boolean`
- `state?: "default" | "paused" | "error"` (default `"default"`)
- `size?: "sm" | "md" | "lg"` (default `"md"`)
